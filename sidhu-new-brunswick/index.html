Know Your Rights

What Insurance Companies Do — And How We Stop Them

New Brunswick insurers are skilled at protecting their bottom line. Here's how they try to reduce your payout — and exactly how we fight back.

Tactic #1

They call quickly and offer a fast settlement

Right after your accident, they reach out with a "fair" offer before you fully understand the extent of your injuries or losses.

We document everything first. We never let you settle before you know the full picture of your damages.

Tactic #2

They record your statement and use it against you

A recorded statement taken while you're in shock can be twisted to minimize your claim or suggest pre-existing conditions.

We advise you before any statement is given. We're with you every step to prevent costly mistakes.

Tactic #3

They delay, hoping you'll give up

Prolonging the process is a calculated strategy. The longer it takes, the more likely you are to accept a lower offer out of desperation.

We keep relentless pressure on insurers with deadlines, documentation, and court filings when necessary.

Tactic #4

They blame you for the accident

Even when you're clearly not at fault, they'll look for any angle to shift blame and reduce how much they owe you.

We build an airtight case with evidence, expert witnesses, and accident reconstruction if needed.
