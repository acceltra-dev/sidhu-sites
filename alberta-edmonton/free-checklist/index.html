The Alberta Accident Checklist

The exact steps to take after a crash in Edmonton to protect your health and your claim, so you recover the maximum compensation you're owed.

  • Get to safety and call 911 if anyone is injured. Report the collision to police.
  • Photograph everything — vehicles, damage, plates, the scene, road and weather.
  • Exchange details — names, licence, registration, and insurance of every driver.
  • Get witness names and numbers before anyone leaves.
  • See a doctor right away, even if you feel fine. Keep every record and receipt.
  • Don't admit fault at the scene or to any adjuster.
  • Stay off social media about the accident or your injuries.
  • Don't give a recorded statement or accept any offer before getting advice.
  • Write down what happened while it's fresh — time, location, sequence.
  • Call a lawyer for a free review before you sign anything.
